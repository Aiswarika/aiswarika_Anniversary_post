Anniversary Post - It has been 4 Years!

Happy 4th anniversary of the organisation. It has been a successful journey because of you.

2021 marks the 4th Anniversary of GirlScript.

We have been working hard to change lives through challenge and discovery. And now, it's time to celebrate! All of you played a crucial role in the enduring history of the organisation and it has been an exquisite journey with you all. We encourage you to participate in the annual event of GirlScript this year. May you keep proving how much of an asset you are. Best wishes on the anniversary.

About

GirlScript is a section-8 NON PROFIT company registered under the government of India on the 2nd of June, 2017. GirlScript is open for all organisation and supports beginners in technology and programming. It supports diversity by reserving 50% seats for women & IGBT in all it's programs worldwide.
